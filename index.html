About Me

I'm someone who's genuinely curious about how things work, whether it's uncovering patterns in data or building web applications that solve real problems. My journey started with Data Analytics, where I developed a passion for transforming raw data into meaningful insights using SQL, Python, and Power BI. As I kept learning, I realized I wanted to go beyond analyzing systems, I wanted to build them too. That curiosity led me into Web Development, where I enjoy creating modern, user-focused applications. I'm currently a fourth-year Business student, and I believe the best part of technology is that there's always something new to learn. Whether it's a new framework, a better development practice, or a more efficient way to analyze data, I'm always excited to dive deeper. Today, I'm growing in both Data and Web Development, combining analytical thinking with problem-solving to create solutions that are both functional and impactful.
